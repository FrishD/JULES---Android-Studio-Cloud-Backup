JULES - Android Studio Cloud Backup

JULES (JetBrains User-Level Encrypted Storage) is a secure cloud backup plugin for Android Studio. It provides automated, AES-256 encrypted backups of your project files directly to your personal Google Drive.

  • Automated Snapshots: Every 9 minutes (configurable).
  • Secure: Local AES-256 encryption.
  • Efficient: Only changes are uploaded (Delta-sync).

Developed by Shay Frishwasser

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

JULES is a client-side application. We do not have servers and do not collect your data.

Scopes Used: We use drive.file and drive.appdata to manage your backups. We only access files created by JULES. Authentication is handled via Google OAuth 2.0; your password is never seen by the plugin.
