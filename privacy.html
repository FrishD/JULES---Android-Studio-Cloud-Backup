Privacy Policy for JULES

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

1. Introduction

JULES is a client-side only application. We do not have backend servers, and we do not collect or sell your personal data.

2. Google Drive Access

The plugin requests restricted permissions (drive.file and drive.appdata) to manage backups. We only access files created by JULES.

3. Data Security

All archives are encrypted locally using AES-256 before transmission. Data flows directly to Google Drive.
